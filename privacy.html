Privacy Policy - ArXiv Toolkit

Effective Date: 2025-03-19

1. Data Collection and Usage

ArXiv Toolkit is a browser extension that runs entirely within your browser. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All features, such as paper filtering and keyword highlighting, operate locally within the browser without processing or sharing user data.

2. Permissions

The extension only requests the minimum necessary permissions for its functionality:

  • Storage: Used to locally store user preferences, such as keyword lists and filtering settings, ensuring a consistent user experience.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Since ArXiv Toolkit does not collect any data, it does not share, sell, or transmit any user information to third parties.

4. Policy Changes and Updates

If this privacy policy is updated, users will be notified through extension updates. You can always review the latest privacy policy on our official website or extension settings page.
